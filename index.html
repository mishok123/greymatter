Service Line 01

Media Intelligence

Know what's being said — across every platform, in every language that matters.

Real-Time Media Monitoring & Sentiment Tracking

Continuous monitoring across print, broadcast, online, and social platforms. We track narrative shifts as they happen — including coverage from thousands of regional-language outlets that global platforms do not reach — and deliver sentiment analysis calibrated for local context.

Stakeholder & Influencer Mapping

Identify the voices shaping opinion in your market. We map the media landscape — journalists, commentators, institutional sources — so you know whose coverage drives perception and where pressure is most likely to emerge.

Crisis Monitoring & Reputation Management

Early warning systems for reputational threats. We surface negative narratives before they escalate, track how a story evolves across media cycles, and provide the situational awareness needed to respond with confidence.

Communications Strategy & Narrative Analysis

Beyond monitoring — we assess how your messaging is landing, how competitors are positioning themselves, and where the strategic narrative opportunities lie.

Media Intelligence tells you what is being said.
Risk Intelligence tells you what it means for your business.

Service Line 02

Risk Intelligence

Understand the environment before you commit — political, regulatory, trade, and operational.

Political & Regulatory Risk

Track shifts in government policy, regulatory frameworks, and institutional direction. We monitor legislative pipelines, ministry announcements, and political dynamics that carry material implications for business operations and investment decisions — translating signal from noise for executives who cannot afford to be surprised.

Trade & Investment Risk

Analyse tariff exposure, bilateral trade agreement developments, supply chain vulnerabilities, and market access conditions. As trade policy becomes increasingly volatile, we provide the context corporates and investors need to stress-test their exposure and plan accordingly.

Geopolitical Risk

South Asia sits at the convergence of competing regional and global interests. We track the geopolitical dynamics — India-Bangladesh-Myanmar relations, US-China strategic competition, multilateral institution positioning — that shape the operating environment for businesses with regional exposure.

Entity Due Diligence

Background intelligence on companies, ownership structures, key individuals, and business relationships. Drawing on local-language sources and primary research, our due diligence reports provide the depth that off-the-shelf database searches cannot.
